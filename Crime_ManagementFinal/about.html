Crime Analysis is a records management and crime analysis system. The aim of the project is to develop a reporting and management system which is easily accessible to t t citizen, police department and the administrative department. This is used to record crime information updated by administrator, station admin and police officer. This software is helpful for law enforcement and citizen safety agencies. It includes all of the tools an agency needs to properly record, store, and retrieve record information from the software.

Administrator

In this module Administrator creates new stations and provide, user ID & Password The Administrator has all the rights to view the complaint status and officer status of every Police station that are under his city control.

Higher Officer

Provide Authentication To staff members: status of each officer.

Police

The duties of a police officer, also known as a law enforcement officer, focus on protecting people and property. They patrol the areas they are assigned, which sometimes include entire jurisdictions, respond to calls, enforce laws, make arrests, issue citations, and occasionally testify in court cases.

User

The user has to register before giving an online complaint. Once the registration is done he can apply for the complaint after which Complaint Id will be sent to his mobile number through which he can view the status of his complaint.
